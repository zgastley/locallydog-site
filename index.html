Local Dog

Three-piece out of Atlanta.

Local Dog is a three-piece band based in Atlanta, GA. We’ve been playing together for years, which gives us the trust on stage to do something cool—mixing genres, stretching songs, and improvising as much as possible.

  • Jeff Jani: Drums
  • Jeff Kujan: Guitar, samples, vocals
  • Zach Gastley: Bass, samples, vocals

We’re still in the lab.

New studio recordings are on the way. Until then, this live session is our favorite snapshot.
